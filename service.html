Food & Drinks

Whether you're a foodie, sommelier or just looking to sample some of the country's finest cuisine, plan your next trip to experience American New Orleans restaurants. A food lover's paradise, New Orleans French-Creole cuisine has a rich history, featuring a vast fusion of many cultures and flavors. This city is a diverse playground of award-winning restaurants that are sure to satisfy any foodie's dream. Try local favorites such as gumbo, jambalaya, and po'boys and wash it all down with traditional Sazerac cocktails, chicory coffee, or a Hurricane. Top it off by indulging in some desserts - beignets, King Cake, or banana's foster are just a few of New Orleans' mouthwatering treats.
