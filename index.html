Host Your Next Event With Style at Our Hotel

Set within a historic Beaux Arts building on the Westbank, Chateau Pichon features over 48,000 sq. ft. of venue space including a Grand Ballroom, two courtyards and the intimate Library Lounge. Whether hosting an impressive convention or an elegant wedding with reception, our Westbank hotel offers the perfect infusion of New Orleans style and flair. Let our Ladies and Gentlemen create a seamless experience that can only be expected from Chateau Pichon.

Retreat To Tranquility

Immerse yourself in the opulent ambiance of Chateau Pichon, adorned with exquisite furnishings and plush bedding, and breathtaking views of the city, creating an oasis of tranquility in the heart of the Westbank.

What's Nearby?

Located on the edge of the Westbank of New Orleans not to far from the world-famous Canal Street, our luxury hotel offers a desirable address just a minutes away from Bourbon Street and Saenger Theatre, host to several top touring attractions.
